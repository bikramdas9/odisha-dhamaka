— Our Story —

Born in Puri,
Made for You

Odisha Dhamaka started in a home kitchen in Bhubaneswar with one mission — serve the warmth of Odia home cooking to people far from home.

Every dish is prepared fresh with locally sourced ingredients, following recipes handed down from grandmothers in the lanes of Puri and Cuttack.

  • ✅ No artificial colours or preservatives
  • ✅ Freshly cooked every day by order
  • ✅ Hygienically packed, delivered within 45 mins
  • ✅ 100% vegetarian kitchen available on request
